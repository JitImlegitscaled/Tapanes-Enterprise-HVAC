Our Core Promise

1-Year Workmanship Guarantee

Every installation, repair, and service we perform is backed by a full one-year workmanship guarantee. If anything we worked on isn't performing as it should within a year of service, we return and make it right — no charges, no questions asked. Your comfort and confidence in our work is non-negotiable.

What the Guarantee Covers

HVAC Installations

Full system installs — split systems, package units, air handlers, and more.

Repairs & Diagnostics

Any repair work performed by our technicians is covered against workmanship defects.

Ductwork & Air Distribution

Duct installations, sealing, and modifications we perform.

Preventive Maintenance

Tune-up and maintenance work performed by our certified team.

Note: The workmanship guarantee covers labor and installation quality. Manufacturer warranties on parts and equipment are separate and provided directly by the equipment manufacturer.

Our Licenses & Certifications

Florida-Licensed HVAC Contractor

Tapanes Enterprise LLC holds a valid Florida HVAC contractor license issued by the Florida Department of Business & Professional Regulation (DBPR). We operate fully within state regulatory requirements — you are always protected.

Fully Insured

We carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. Every job we perform protects you, your property, and our team — from the first visit through completion.

EPA Section 608 Certified

Our technicians hold EPA 608 certification for the safe handling, recovery, and management of refrigerants. This federal certification ensures our work meets environmental standards and protects both your home and the environment.

Ongoing Professional Training

Our team participates in ongoing manufacturer and industry training programs to stay current with the latest HVAC technology, refrigerants, and best practices — ensuring every job is done right the first time.

100% Satisfaction Commitment

We don't consider a job complete until you're fully satisfied. Every service call ends with a walkthrough — we explain exactly what was done, why, and what to expect going forward. There are no surprise charges, no vague diagnoses, and no pressure tactics.

Transparent pricing, honest diagnostics, and clear communication are our standard — not an upsell.
