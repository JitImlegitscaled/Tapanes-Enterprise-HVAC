Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Call us at 813-578-1529 — we're happy to help.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?
We recommend at least twice a year — once in spring before the cooling season, and once in fall before the heating season. Regular tune-ups keep your system efficient, catch small problems early, and help extend equipment life.
Do you service both homes and businesses?
Yes. Tapanes Enterprise provides HVAC services for both residential and commercial clients throughout the Tampa Bay area. Whether it's a single-family home or a commercial office, we can help.
What HVAC brands do you work on?
We service all major HVAC brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin, American Standard, Bryant, and more. If you own it, we can service it.
Do I get a quote before work begins?
Always. We diagnose the issue, explain what we found, and provide a clear upfront quote before any work begins. You approve the work — we never start without your agreement.
What signs mean my AC needs repair?
Common signs include: weak or inconsistent airflow, warm air blowing instead of cool, unusual noises (banging, rattling, squealing), unexplained energy bill spikes, frequent on/off cycling, or ice buildup on the unit. If you notice any of these, get it looked at before it becomes a larger issue.
